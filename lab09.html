Analysis

I found that Trumps highest approval ratings happened in the first 3 months of his presidency, with a huge spike on the 25th day of January. It is expected that his approval rating is high his first few months, which matches the graph, but most presidents keep generally high approval ratings for at least their first year. I also noticed Trump hit an all time low in approval ratings on August 4th, which I labeled on the graph. Trumps approval rating is rising now, which is also a trend for most presidents as most approval ratings tend to revert back to 50 percent in the long run.
